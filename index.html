Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is this a replacement for an immigration lawyer?
No. I work on your business plan and endorsement strategy, not legal advice. For visa application paperwork or appeals, work with a qualified immigration solicitor. The two roles complement each other.
What does an engagement look like?
Most engagements run 1–4 weeks, mixing async work and 1–2 working sessions. I review your plan against the actual endorsement criteria, flag the gaps, and we rebuild the weak sections together so the plan you submit is the one you want endorsed.
Will you write my plan from scratch?
No. The plan has to be yours. Endorsing bodies are good at spotting ghostwritten work, and an interview will catch you out anyway. I help you make your plan one that gets endorsed.
Can you help if I've already been rejected?
Yes. Rejected-applicant rebuilds are one of the most common engagements. We analyse the rejection letter, reverse-engineer what failed, and rebuild the plan against the body's actual scoring lens before you resubmit.
Do you work with applicants outside the UK?
Yes. Calls run on Zoom or Google Meet, time-zone permitting. The endorsing-body lens I apply is the same wherever you're based.
What does pricing look like?
Engagements start from £499 for tight-scope reviews. Each is quoted individually after the free 30-min discovery call. No retainers, no monthly contracts. You see the full price before you commit.
Is my plan kept confidential?
Yes. Plans are never shared, never used for marketing, never used to train models. Your idea stays yours.
